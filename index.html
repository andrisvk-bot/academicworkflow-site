The Intensive

This is not generic AI advice. It is a disciplined performance system, applied to your course and your assignments.

1) Brief to blueprint

Turn any assignment brief into a clear structure, including sections, argument flow, and marking focus.

2) Research acceleration

Extract themes, comparisons, and critical angles quickly. Reduce overwhelm and improve direction.

3) Argument scaffolding

Move from descriptive writing to analytical depth using a repeatable structure that improves clarity.

4) Ethical AI workflow

Use AI as a thinking and refinement tool, not a substitution. Stay aligned with academic conduct.

5) Editing pipeline

Improve logic, reduce fluff, strengthen transitions, and ensure the writing reads clearly and academically.

6) Personal prompt architecture

Build a prompt set that matches your course style and your assignment type, so outputs stay consistent.
