Ethics and academic conduct

This service develops your reasoning process. It does not replace your authorship.

What this service does

  • Teaches structured workflows for research, planning, and editing
  • Keeps you in control of content and decisions
  • Aligns AI use with academic conduct expectations

What this service does not do

  • Write assignments for submission
  • Encourage academic misconduct
  • Guarantee grades

Note: rules vary by university, but the boundary is consistent. AI supports thinking and refinement, not replacement.
